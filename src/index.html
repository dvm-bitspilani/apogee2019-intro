CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

APOGEE, the technical festival of BITS Pilani, is in its 37th year now and is bigger than ever. Considered to be one of the biggest platforms to discover, nurture and accelerate fresh ideas from bright minds all over the country, this festival attracts more than 8000 students, spanning over a 100 colleges. As a campus ambassador, you would be responsible for the outreach and publicity of the festival. Simply put, you would be the face of the festival as well as BITS itself for the students in your college. Become a campus ambassador to stand a chance to work with for one of our awesome sponsors, get LoRs, Goodies, Passes and much more!
